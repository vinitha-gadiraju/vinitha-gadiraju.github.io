Hi! I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Colorado, Boulder (CU Boulder). I work with Dr. Shaun Kane in the Superhuman Computing Lab to develop educational tools to help visually impaired children and members of their support network (family members, friends, educators) learn Braille and Independent Living Skills. I am particularly passionate about education equity, learning through play, and cross-ability collaboration.

I am excited to chat more with you!
