Hi! I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Colorado, Boulder (CU Boulder). I work with Dr. Shaun Kane in the Superhuman Computing Lab to develop educational tools to help visually impaired children and members of their support network (family members, friends, educators) learn Braille and social skills. I am particularly passionate about education equity and the intersection of bilingual/immigrant backgrounds with instruction for blind or visually impaired children and their families.
