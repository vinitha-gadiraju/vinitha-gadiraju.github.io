Hi! I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Colorado, Boulder (CU Boulder) working with Dr. Shaun Kane in the Superhuman Computing Lab.

I develop educational tools to help visually impaired children and members of their support network (family members, friends, educators) learn Braille and Independent Living Skills.

I am passionate about education equity, learning through play, and cross-ability collaboration.
